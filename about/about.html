📝 My To-Do App

A simple, clean, and effective way to manage your daily tasks and goals.

🚀 Features

  • Create and manage your to-do lists easily
  • Mark tasks as complete
  • Organize tasks by category
  • Offline support
  • Minimal and intuitive interface

📱 How to Use

  • Tap the + button to add a new task
  • Swipe left or tap to mark a task as done
  • Tap a task to edit or delete
  • Use filters to view completed, pending, or all tasks

💡 Tips

  • Use short titles for quick glance
  • Break big tasks into subtasks
  • Review your list at the start and end of your day
