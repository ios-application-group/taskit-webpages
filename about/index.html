📝 TaskIt

Organize Your Life, One Checklist at a Time. TaskIt is your ultimate productivity companion. Whether you're managing a complex project or tracking your daily chores, TaskIt gives you the flexibility to organize everything exactly how you want it.

🚀 Key Features

  • 🗂️ Custom Boards: Create dedicated boards for work, personal life, or specific projects to keep your tasks perfectly categorized.
  • ✅ Hierarchical Task Management: Add tasks to your boards, and dive deeper with detailed checklist items for each task.
  • 📅 Smart Scheduling & Recurring Tasks: Schedule due dates for items, or configure them to repeat daily or on specific custom dates.
  • ⚡ Priority Tracking: Assign priority levels to your checklist items so you always know what to tackle first.
  • 📝 Rich Context: Add detailed notes and comments directly to your checklist items to keep all important information in one place.
  • 🗑️ Complete Control: Enjoy full flexibility with the ability to easily edit, manage, or delete boards, tasks, and items whenever you need.

📱 How to Use

  • Create a Board for your project or category.
  • Add Tasks inside your selected board.
  • Break tasks down by adding detailed Checklist Items.
  • Customize items with dates, priorities, notes, and comments to fit your workflow.
  • Delete boards, tasks, or items seamlessly whenever your plans change.
