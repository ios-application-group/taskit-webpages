Getting Started

1

Login with Apple (Optional)

Start by signing in with your Apple ID for a seamless and secure experience. This helps keep your tasks synced across all your devices. You can also skip this and start immediately offline!

2

Create Your First Board

Boards help you separate different areas of your life (e.g., 'Work', 'Groceries', 'Personal'). Tap the + icon on the home screen to create a new board and pick a custom color for it.

3

Add Tasks & Checklists

Inside a board, add your main tasks. Then, tap on a task to dive deeper and add specific Checklist Items. This is where the magic happens!

4

Schedule & Prioritize

When creating or editing a checklist item, look for the calendar and flag icons:

  • Schedule: Pick a date and time, or set the task to repeat daily, weekly, or custom dates.
  • Priority: Assign High, Medium, or Low priority so you know what to focus on first.
